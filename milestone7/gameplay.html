Titanfall 2 from a gameplay perspective was not much different from the games that came out at the time. Most of them being jetpack futuristic movement FPS games. But what I think set Titanfall above the rest was the sheer amount of different ways to play and experience the game. The fans of this game have stuck with it through thick and thin. The best example being how EA treated this gem of a title. The fact that I can go to youtube and look at semi-recent content of people who are just recently discovering the game wishing they would have gave this game a chance earlier.

The function above is a little something I like to call the Titanfall Player Experience. Now this seems a bit odd but it has nothing to do with the game itself. It has to do with the constant lack of help from the developers of the game. EA, who dropped their most popular AAA game title Battlefield One.

On top of that facing multiple DDOS and Hacking attacks making the game unplayable for a time. Until our amazing community made their own dedicated servers strictly for multiplayer. Allowing people to enjoy this amazing game in its once amazing prime, even having updates as well by the people who owned the servers.

Early this year I believe Titanfall 2 had some what of a comeback and still to this day has a pretty good amount of players for an almost decade old game. With that surge in popularity some of the developers of Titanfall did have some service to the game but did not last as they again moved on.
